Our Facilities Provided

Laboratory:

Holy Spirit School offers five well-equipped labs for hands-on learning in science subjects:Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Composite Lab, and Viva Room.

NCC

It's offers NCC training with skilled teachers. Senior secondary subjects include Science, Arts, and Commerce streams

Library

The library holds over 4,000 books, magazines, and newspapers. A dedicated activity room is available for pre-primary students.

Computer lab

The computer lab has 40 computers, AC, UPS, and a projector. Modern teaching methods and trained teachers ensure an engaging learning experience.

Playground

Wide playground that accommodates for playing football, cricket, volleyball, kho-kho, kabaddi etc.
